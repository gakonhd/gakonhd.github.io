On the chosen date and time, the procression of Dang's family will come to Hannah's house with wedding cars, flowers and offerings.

The permission ceremony begins in front of the bride's ancestral altar. Dang and Hannah, with their respective parents, burn incense sticks, asking for permission from the ancestors to bless them. The couple turns and bows to their parents, gives thanks for raising and protecting them. The bride and groom will then bow to each other.

Once stepping on the weddingcar to groom’s house, from the customary perspective, Hannah’s officially left behind her single life.
